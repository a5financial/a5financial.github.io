Super Visa Insurance.

A Super Visa is a special type of long-term visitor visa for parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens or permanent residents. It allows them to stay in Canada for extended periods without needing frequent renewals.

What is a Super Visa?

A Super Visa is a multi-entry visa that allows parents and grandparents to visit Canada for extended periods, providing family members with long-term support and peace of mind.

Super Visa Features

Here are the key features of the Super Visa that makes it a valuable option for parents and grandparents visiting Canada.

Feature Description
Valid for up to 10 years The Super Visa allows a multi-entry visa valid for up to 10 years.
Each entry allows up to 5 years Each entry permits a stay of up to 5 years, with the possibility of extensions.
Private medical insurance required Applicants must purchase private medical insurance from a Canadian insurance provider.
Eligibility requirements Applicants must meet additional eligibility criteria to qualify for the Super Visa.

Why does a client need a Super Visa?

Reason Description
Long Family Visits Parents and grandparents can spend significant time with family without applying for visitor status repeatedly.
Stability and Convenience Because the visa is valid for years, travel in and out of Canada is easier.
Financial Protection Mandatory medical insurance ensures visitors are covered for healthcare, protecting families from high medical costs.
Ideal for Growing Families Helpful when families need support after childbirth, childcare help, or simply want parents close by.

Simple Way to Explain to Clients

A Super Visa allows your parents or grandparents to stay with you in Canada for several years at a time instead of just a few months. It provides peace of mind because they must have medical insurance, so you’re protected from unexpected healthcare expenses.
