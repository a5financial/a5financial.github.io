Meet Our Founder, Paramjeet (Param) Kaur Brar

Param is the founder and CEO of A5 Financial Ltd., with over five years of experience in the life insurance and investment industry. She is known for her client focused approach and technical expertise, delivering trusted financial solutions to individuals, families, and businesses across Ontario.

As a licensed paralegal and a graduate of Humber College's Life License Qualification Program (LLQP), Param has built a solid reputation by offering tailored financial services. Prior to A5 Financial Ltd., she gained valuable experience in the immigration industry, where she developed exceptional client service skills.

In addition to her professional expertise, Param enjoys Gidha, Bhangra, folk music, and has a passion for numerology and Vastu, continually expanding her knowledge in these areas.
