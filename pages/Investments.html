• Tax-free withdrawals for home purchase
• $8,000/year contribution limit, $40,000 lifetime cap 		• Major tax savings

• Ideal for young professionals & new immigrants 		The FHSA is a powerful tool for first-time homebuyers, offering tax savings now, tax-free growth, and tax-free withdrawals for buying a home.
RRSP (Registered Retirement Savings Plan) A retirement savings plan offering tax advantages for Canadians. • Tax-deductible contributions
• Tax-deferred growth
• Taxable upon withdrawal in retirement
• Flexible contribution limits 		• Immediate tax savings
• Tax-deferred growth
• Structured retirement planning
• Flexible contributions 		Contribute to an RRSP to reduce your taxable income now, while growing your savings for retirement tax-deferred.
TFSA (Tax-Free Savings Account) A flexible savings and investment account with tax-free growth. • $6,500/year contribution limit
• Tax-free growth
• Tax-free withdrawals
• Flexibility for short or long-term goals 		• Tax-free growth
• Flexible withdrawals
